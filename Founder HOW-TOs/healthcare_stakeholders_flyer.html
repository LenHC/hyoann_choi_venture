Deep tech commercialization · 01

Healthcare has three different buyers — and they're rarely the same person

Before you build, know exactly who you're selling to, who's writing the check, and who's approving coverage.

User

Doctor / Patient

Who actually uses your product

"Does this make my job easier or improve my outcome?"

Customer

Hospital / GPO / Purchaser

Who signs the contract and pays

"Does this reduce cost, liability, or improve throughput?"

Payer

Insurance / CMS / Employer

Who approves reimbursement

"Is there clinical evidence and a billing code for this?"

The key insight

A doctor can love your device and a hospital can buy it — but if the payer won't reimburse, no one will use it at scale. Winning all three requires different evidence, different conversations, and different timing.

Real-world example: continuous glucose monitor

User

Diabetic patient / endocrinologist

Customer

Pharmacy / durable medical equipment supplier

Payer

Medicare, Medicaid, or private insurer
